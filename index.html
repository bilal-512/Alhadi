About the iHadi Quranic Institute

At iHadi Quranic Institute, we are dedicated to delivering authentic and accessible online Quran education to students of all ages across the globe. Our mission is to spread the light of the Holy Quran through structured online Quran courses, including Quran reading, Tajweed, memorization (Hifz), and Islamic studies. Guided by qualified and experienced scholars, we provide personalized one-on-one sessions that cater to both children and adults. Whether you’re a beginner or looking to strengthen your Quranic recitation, iHadi is your trusted platform to learn the Quran online with spiritual depth and academic excellence.

Why Choose Us?

At iHadi Quranic Institute, we stand out by offering expert-led, personalized online Quran learning with certified male and female tutors. Our flexible class schedules, interactive one-on-one sessions, and student-centered approach make it easy for learners of all ages to learn the Quran online at their own pace. With a structured curriculum covering Quran reading, Tajweed, Hifz, and Islamic studies, we ensure spiritual growth and academic excellence.
