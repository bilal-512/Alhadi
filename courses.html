Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan later?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will take effect from the next billing cycle.
Is there a refund policy?
We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied within the first week, we'll provide a full refund.
Are there any hidden fees?
No, our pricing is transparent. The displayed price includes all features mentioned in the plan. No hidden charges.
Do you offer family discounts?
Yes! We offer 15% discount for families with 2+ students and 20% discount for 3+ students. Contact us for details.

Don't worry! You can reschedule missed classes with 24-hour notice. We offer flexible scheduling to accommodate your needs.
